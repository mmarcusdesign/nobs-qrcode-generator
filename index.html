Why Use No B.S. QR?

We built this tool because most QR generators are annoying. They ask for your email, force you to sign up, or break your codes after a "free trial."

We don't do that. This tool runs entirely in your browser. We don't see your data, we don't track your links, and we don't charge you.

Privacy First

Because this tool uses client-side technology, your input data never leaves your device. It is not sent to our servers. It is the safest way to generate codes for sensitive information.
