FAQ: Troubleshooting Your QR Codes

While our generator is reliable, here are common issues and quick tips to ensure your codes always scan perfectly:

  • **Why won't my code scan?** The most common reason is too much data (a very long URL). Use a URL shortener if possible. Also, ensure there is sufficient contrast (the quiet zone) around the code.
  • **What is the best file format?** For high-quality printing where resizing is required (e.g., posters), always use the **EPS (Vector/SVG)** option. For web and digital use, PNG is ideal.
  • **Does the color matter?** Most scanners require high contrast. While black and white is safest, if you use color, ensure the foreground color is much darker than the background color.
  • **What is the 'quiet zone'?** This is the mandatory white border around the QR code that allows the scanner to detect the edges. Do not crop it out!
