Frequently Asked Questions

We're compiling a list of the most common questions. In the meantime, here are a few basics:

Q: Is this tool really free?

A: Yes, all tools on AuroHue Design Tools are 100% free to use, forever. We don't believe in charging for simple utilities.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. All the core tools (QR, Color, Ipsum, Ratio) run entirely in your web browser. Your input data never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers.

Q: How do you make money?

A: We run privacy-friendly display advertisements (like the ones you see in the code) to cover hosting costs and keep the tools free for everyone.
