# **Privacy Policy for SimpleQR Maker**

**Effective Date: December 4, 2025**  
Welcome to SimpleQR Maker (accessible at https://www.simpleqrmaker.net/). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect and how we use it.

## **1\. Our No B.S. Policy on QR Code Data**

**We do not store, log, or transmit the content you enter to generate QR codes.**

* **Client-Side Processing:** The core function of SimpleQR Maker—generating the QR code image from your text or URL—is performed entirely within your web browser (client-side). Your input data (the URL, email, text, or phone number) never leaves your device to be processed or stored on our servers.  
* **Data Security:** Because we never store your input, there is no risk of this sensitive content being compromised in a data breach on our end.

## **2\. Information We Collect**

We only collect non-personal information through third-party services necessary for maintaining and funding the site.

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visit to this and other sites on the Internet.

* **Data Collected by AdSense:** AdSense may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the content of the ad you were shown. This data is used by Google to tailor advertising and measure ad effectiveness.  
* **Your Control:** You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page.

### **b. Anonymous Analytics**

We may use general, privacy-focused analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand basic usage patterns, like how many people visit the site, what countries they are from, and which features are most popular.

* **Data Collected:** Page views, duration of visit, and device type (desktop/mobile).  
* **Data Exclusion:** We specifically configure our analytics to **exclude** any identifiable information and do not link site usage to specific individuals.
